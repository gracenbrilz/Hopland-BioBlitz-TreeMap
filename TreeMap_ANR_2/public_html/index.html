Species List
The Hopland BioBlitz recorded through iNaturalist

This tree map shows the number of specimens recorded during the Hopland BioBlitz -- a weekend event where scientists and researchers find, identify and voucher (as appropriate) as many plant and animal species as possible at the Hopland Research and Extension Center. The Hopland Research and Extension Center is a beautiful 5,300-acre site with oak woodlands, rangeland, grassland, chaparral, riparian environments and pockets of mixed conifer forests. Specimens are recorded using iNaturalist, and this tool collects and displays the live data (updated every 15 minutes)!
